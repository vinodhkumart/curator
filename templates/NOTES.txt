CronJob {{ template "curator.name" . }} has been installed in the cluster.

Get the CronJob by running these commands:

$ kubectl get cronjob --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l "app={{ template "curator.name" . }}, release={{ .Release.Name }}"

To see previous jobs run:

$ kubectl get jobs --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l "app={{ template "curator.name" . }}, release={{ .Release.Name }}"

To get logs of the previous pod run:

$ export POD_NAME=$(kubectl get pods --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l "app={{ template "curator.name" . }}, release={{ .Release.Name }}" --show-all -o jsonpath="{.items[0].metadata.name}")
$ kubectl logs --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} POD_NAME
